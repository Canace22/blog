## Summary

Vocabulary cheat sheet: English verbs common in coding prompts (develop, edit, create, explain, design, refactor, etc.) with Chinese glosses, short explanations, and example sentences for use when writing instructions to models.

## Key Points

- Organized as numbered entries: original word, translation, explanation, example sentence.
- Mix of general software verbs (implement, update, extract) and process words (analyze, clarify, revise).
- Includes a couple of Chinese→English glosses at the end (e.g. 策略 → strategy, 要求 → requirement).
